Just sharing some additional information related to welding machine safety. Normally we install RCCB at the input side of the welding machines to prevent electrical shock to any person but we forget output side, some times accident (electrical shock) may happen at output side also. RCCB installed at input side will not help in preventing electrical shock to anyone at output side of the welding machines. Although the electrical shock at out side are very rare but accident happens . To prevent the electrical shock at outside we use VRD voltage reduction device . Now a days welding machines comes with inbuilt VRD but you can buy separate VRD unit also and fix it with the old welding machines as well. When welding will perform welding this VRD will remain inactive but the moment welder will remove the electrode holder from the welding action, VRD will reduce the output voltage to 12 V. It means when welding holder is idle it will remain at 12V and safe.
